ISSUE 01

Trading On Chart Alone

Most retail traders open a chart, draw a few lines, and guess. The chart doesn't tell you where the big money is sitting. Chart-only trading is the fastest way to the wrong side of a move.

ISSUE 02

No Macro Layer

Rate expectations, COT positioning, sentiment. The three data layers that drive 80% of directional bias are absent from most retail workflows. Price alone is a lagging signal.

ISSUE 03

Risk Management Is An Afterthought

Position sizing gets picked by gut feel, not math. One bad week wipes out six months of discipline because stops were set wrong from the start.
